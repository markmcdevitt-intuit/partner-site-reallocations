PHASE 1 — Partner-Level Reallocation

Unscheduled hours reallocation logic

UR-001  /  UR-002

Each partner has independently configurable release rules. When a rule fires, that partner's unscheduled hours are contributed to a shared pool. The pool is then distributed equally across all partners in the workgroup — giving each an identical share of the rebalanced capacity.
